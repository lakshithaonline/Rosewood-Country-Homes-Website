Pink Rose

Our luxurious two-story house, Pink Rose, offers a tranquil oasis in the heart of a bustling neighborhood. With a stylish, modern design and all the amenities you need to feel at home, it's the perfect base for exploring all that Sri Lanka has to offer.

DESCRIPTION

This spacious house features three bedrooms, each with its own private bathroom, a fully equipped kitchen, a large living area, and a beautiful outdoor space with a pool and garden. Whether you're traveling with family or friends, Pink Rose is the ideal retreat for a relaxing and memorable vacation in Sri Lanka.
