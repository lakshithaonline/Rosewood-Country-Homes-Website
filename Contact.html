Who we are

Rosewood Country Homes is a hospitality company dedicated to providing guests with the ultimate Sri Lankan experience. We believe that a great stay starts with a great home away from home, and that's why we've created a collection of luxurious properties in some of Sri Lanka's most stunning locations. Our mission is to provide our guests with the highest level of comfort and personalized service, and to create lasting memories that will keep them coming back to Sri Lanka time and time again.

Vision and Mission

Our vision at Rosewood Country Homes is to provide guests with an unforgettable vacation experience in Sri Lanka, with luxurious accommodations and exceptional service. 

Our mission is to exceed our guests' expectations and create long-lasting memories by offering unique properties in breathtaking locations, personalized attention to each guest's needs, and a commitment to sustainable tourism practices. We strive to make our guests feel at home and to showcase the beauty and culture of Sri Lanka through our properties and services.
